9Damage & Accidents

You must inspect the vehicle thoroughly before departure and report any existing damage to our representative. Unreported pre-existing damage may be charged to the renter upon return.

In the event of an accident:

  • Ensure the safety of all parties and contact emergency services if required

  • Notify SmartDrive Pro immediately by calling our 24/7 helpline
  • File a police FIR within 24 hours and provide us with a copy

Damage to the vehicle beyond normal wear and tear will be assessed and charged to the renter. A damage assessment report will be provided within 5 business days.
