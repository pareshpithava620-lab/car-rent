🔒Data Encryption

Every piece of data that travels between your browser or app and our servers is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.3) with 256-bit AES encryption — the same standard used by banks and financial institutions worldwide.

🌐 In-Transit Encryption

All HTTP connections are automatically upgraded to HTTPS. Our SSL certificate is verified by DigiCert, one of the world's most trusted certificate authorities.

🗄️ At-Rest Encryption

Sensitive data stored in our databases — including ID documents, payment details, and personal information — is encrypted at rest using AES-256.

🔑 Key Management

Encryption keys are rotated automatically every 90 days and stored separately from the encrypted data, managed via a dedicated Hardware Security Module (HSM).

📱 App Security

Our mobile app uses certificate pinning to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks, with biometric authentication as an additional security layer.

💳Payment Security

SmartDrive Pro never stores your complete card number, CVV, or payment credentials on our servers. All payment processing is handled by our PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment gateway partners.

  • We use tokenisation — your card details are replaced with a secure token for recurring payments
  • 3D Secure (3DS2) authentication is enforced for all card transactions
  • UPI payments use India's NPCI-regulated infrastructure with end-to-end encryption
  • All transactions are monitored in real-time for suspicious activity
  • Multi-factor authentication is required for large-value bookings above ₹25,000

🏦 Zero Liability Promise: If unauthorised charges appear on your account due to a security breach on our platform, SmartDrive Pro will refund the full amount within 2 business days — no questions asked.

👤Account Security

Protecting your SmartDrive Pro account from unauthorised access is a shared responsibility. Here's what we do on our end, and what we recommend for you:

What We Do

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with per-user salting — we cannot see your password
  • Account lockout after 5 consecutive failed login attempts (unlocks after 30 minutes)
  • Automatic session expiry after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Email notifications for all account logins from new devices
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via SMS or Authenticator app (available and recommended)

What You Should Do

  • Use a strong, unique password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, numbers, and symbols)
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication in your account settings
  • Never share your login credentials, OTPs, or booking confirmation codes with anyone
  • Log out from shared devices immediately after use
  • Report any suspicious activity to security@smartdrivepro.in immediately

Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures staff can only access data relevant to their function. All access is logged and audited.

🚨Reporting a Security Issue

SmartDrive Pro takes security vulnerabilities seriously. If you discover a potential security issue on our platform, we encourage responsible disclosure:

  • Email details of the vulnerability to security@smartdrivepro.in
  • Include a clear description, steps to reproduce, and potential impact
  • Do not access, modify, or delete any user data as part of your research
  • We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours and provide a resolution timeline
  • Responsible disclosures may be eligible for our Security Bug Bounty programme
