📝How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Log in to your SmartDrive Pro account at smartdrivepro.in
  • Step 2: Navigate to "My Bookings" and select the booking you wish to refund
  • Step 3: Click "Request Refund" and fill in the reason and any supporting details
  • Step 4: Submit your request — you'll receive a confirmation email within 30 minutes
  • Step 5: Our team will review and approve your refund within 1 business day
  • Step 6: The refund is initiated to your original payment method within the timeline above

You can also request a refund by emailing refunds@smartdrivepro.in or calling our support team at +91 80001 23456.

🔄Refund vs. Credit Voucher

In some situations, SmartDrive Pro may offer a credit voucher instead of (or in addition to) a monetary refund:

  • Credit vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of issue and can be used for any future booking
  • Vouchers can be applied to any vehicle category across all 25+ cities we operate in
  • If you prefer a monetary refund over a voucher, you can request this explicitly — we will always honour your preference
  • Vouchers cannot be transferred or converted to cash

🔍Refund Disputes

If you believe a refund decision is incorrect or unfair, you have the right to escalate the matter:

  • Level 1: Contact our customer support team at support@smartdrivepro.in — response within 24 hours
  • Level 2: Request escalation to our Refund Review Committee — decision within 5 business days
  • Level 3: If still unresolved, you may file a complaint with the National Consumer Helpline (1800-11-4000) or approach the consumer forum in your city

SmartDrive Pro will never withhold a legitimate refund. Our goal is to ensure every customer leaves satisfied, even when things don't go as planned.
