Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?+
You need a valid driving licence (at least 1 year old), government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport), and a selfie. No lengthy paperwork — the entire verification is digital.
Is there a minimum rental period?+
Our minimum rental period is 12 hours for daily rentals. Weekly and monthly plans start from 7 consecutive days. We also offer outstation and chauffeur-driven options with no minimums.
Are there any hidden charges?+
Absolutely not. We operate on strict transparent pricing. The amount shown during booking is final. Fuel, tolls, and inter-city charges are clearly communicated before confirmation.
What if I need to cancel or modify my booking?+
Free cancellation is available up to 2 hours before the rental start time. Modifications can be made anytime through the app or by calling support. Full refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
Do you offer corporate car rental plans?+
Yes! Our corporate plans include dedicated account managers, monthly billing, custom SLAs, and access to our entire premium fleet. Contact us at bookings@smartdrivepro.in for a custom quote.
