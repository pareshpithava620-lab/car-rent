Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains clearly how SmartDrive Pro collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Summary: SmartDrive Pro collects only the information necessary to provide you with our car rental service. We never sell your personal data to third parties. You have full control over your information at any time.

1Information We Collect

When you use SmartDrive Pro — whether through our website, mobile app, or customer support — we may collect the following categories of personal information:

Personal Identification Information

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Government-issued ID details (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport) for KYC verification
  • Driving licence number, issuing state, and expiry date
  • Selfie/photograph for identity verification purposes

Booking & Payment Information

  • Rental preferences: pickup and return locations, dates, and vehicle type
  • Payment method details (processed securely via our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway — we never store full card numbers)
  • Booking history and transaction records

Device & Usage Information

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation path
  • Referring URLs and search terms used to find our platform

2How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information strictly for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Processing your rental booking and delivering the vehicle
  • Verifying your identity and driving licence as legally required
  • Processing payments and issuing invoices and receipts
  • Communicating booking confirmations, reminders, and important updates
  • Providing 24/7 customer support and resolving disputes
  • Improving our platform features and personalising your experience
  • Complying with applicable Indian laws and regulations
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorised access, and illegal activity

We will only send you marketing communications if you have explicitly opted in. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email or by contacting us directly.

3Data Sharing

SmartDrive Pro does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Fleet Partners: Vehicle owners and fleet operators receive only the information necessary to fulfill your booking (name, contact number, pickup location)
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe) process your payment details under their own privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Insurance Providers: In the event of an accident or claim, relevant booking and driver details may be shared with our insurance partners
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist with IT infrastructure, analytics, and customer communication — all bound by strict confidentiality agreements

4Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our platform
  • AES-256 encryption for data stored in our databases
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all admin access
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • PCI-DSS compliance for all payment processing
  • Role-based access controls — only authorised staff can access sensitive data

Despite our best efforts, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account security has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@smartdrivepro.in.

5Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (login sessions, booking cart)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as language and currency
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (only with your consent)

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our booking system.

6Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws and the Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Right to Complain: Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@smartdrivepro.in. We will respond within 30 days.

7Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, and resolve disputes:

  • Active account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 2 years
  • Booking and transaction records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • KYC documents: Retained for 5 years as required by Indian regulations
  • Marketing preferences: Retained until you withdraw consent
  • Website analytics data: Retained for 26 months in anonymised form

8Children's Privacy

SmartDrive Pro is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Our service requires a valid driving licence, which is only issued to adults aged 18 and above in India.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete the data promptly.

9Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service offerings. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an email notification to registered users
  • Display a prominent notice on our website for 30 days

Your continued use of SmartDrive Pro after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
