📱How to Cancel Your Booking

Cancelling your SmartDrive Pro booking is quick and easy. Choose any of the three methods below:

1

Via Website

Log in to your account → My Bookings → Select booking → Click "Cancel Booking"

2

Via Mobile App

Open the SmartDrive Pro app → Bookings → Tap your booking → Cancel & confirm

3

Via Phone / WhatsApp

Call or WhatsApp us at +91 80001 23456. Our team processes cancellations instantly.

You will receive a cancellation confirmation email within 5 minutes. If you don't receive one, please contact our support team.

⚠️Special Cancellation Conditions

Peak Season & Special Events

During major Indian holidays and events (Diwali, New Year, long weekends, IPL season), stricter cancellation terms may apply for select vehicles. These will be clearly displayed during the booking process before you confirm payment.

Wedding & Corporate Packages

Cancellations for multi-vehicle wedding packages or corporate fleet bookings are subject to a separate agreement signed at the time of booking. Generally, cancellations within 7 days of the event date attract a 25% cancellation fee.

Force Majeure

In the event of natural disasters, government-mandated lockdowns, extreme weather, or other circumstances beyond either party's control, SmartDrive Pro will provide a full credit voucher valid for 12 months, or a full refund at your choice.

  • Cancellations due to medical emergencies will be processed with full refunds upon submission of a medical certificate
  • Cancellations due to flight delays or cancellations require supporting documentation from the airline

🔄Booking Modifications

Need to change your booking rather than cancel? Modifications are handled separately:

  • Date changes: Free if made more than 2 hours before pickup, subject to availability
  • Vehicle upgrade: Free upgrade if a better vehicle is available; you pay only the price difference
  • Pickup location change: Free if within the same city; a relocation fee may apply for different cities
  • Extension of rental: Subject to vehicle availability; contact us at least 4 hours before the original return time

Multiple modifications to a single booking may be subject to a ₹299 per-modification fee after the second change.

❓Cancellation by SmartDrive Pro

In rare circumstances, SmartDrive Pro may need to cancel your booking. This may happen due to:

  • Unavailability of the booked vehicle due to unforeseen mechanical issues
  • Natural disasters, government restrictions, or force majeure events
  • Fraud detection or failure to complete KYC verification
  • Repeated no-shows or policy violations on your account

If SmartDrive Pro cancels your booking, you will receive a full refund within 3–5 business days, plus a ₹500 credit voucher as a gesture of goodwill, unless cancellation is due to policy violations on your part.
