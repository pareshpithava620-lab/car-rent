Our Story

Born from a Vision of Smarter Mobility

SmartDrive Pro started in 2019 with a simple belief — renting a car should be as seamless as using any modern app. No paperwork queues, no hidden charges, no uncertainty.

Our founders, frustrated by the traditional rental experience, built a platform that puts the customer first — from discovering the right vehicle to returning it with zero hassle.

Today, we operate in 25+ Indian cities with 500+ verified vehicles — from compact city cars to high-end executive sedans and EVs.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

From a small Mumbai fleet to a pan-India premium car rental brand.

2019

🚀 Founded in Mumbai

SmartDrive Pro launched with 12 premium vehicles. First booking came within 6 hours of going live.

2020

🌆 Expansion to 5 Cities

Expanded to Delhi, Bengaluru, Pune and Hyderabad. Our contactless booking model became a benchmark.

2021

📱 Mobile App Launch

Award-winning mobile app crossed 100,000 downloads in its first month with instant booking and live tracking.

2022

🏆 Best Startup Award

Recognized as India's Best Car Rental Startup at the National Mobility Awards. Fleet grew to 200+ vehicles.

2023

⚡ EV Fleet Launch

India's largest electric car rental sub-fleet — 50+ EVs across 10 cities for sustainable mobility.

2024–25

🌍 Pan-India & 50K+ Customers

25+ cities, 500+ vehicles, 50,000+ happy customers. Dubai expansion planned for Q3 2025.
