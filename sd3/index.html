What documents do I need to rent a car?
+

A valid driving licence (1+ year old), any government-issued ID (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport), and a selfie. International visitors need a valid passport and international driving permit. All verification is 100% digital.

Is there a minimum rental period?
+

Our minimum is 12 hours for daily rentals. Weekly plans start from 7 consecutive days. Hourly rentals (4hr minimum) are available in select cities.

Are there any hidden charges?
+

Absolutely none. The price shown during booking is exactly what you pay. Fuel and tolls are separate as per actual consumption, clearly communicated upfront.

What if I need to cancel or modify my booking?
+

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before pickup — full refund, no questions asked. Modifications are free anytime. Refunds process within 3–5 business days.

Do you offer corporate car rental plans?
+

Yes! Our corporate plans include dedicated account managers, monthly billing, custom SLAs, priority fleet access, and discounted rates. Email bookings@smartdrivepro.in for a tailored quote.
